# Refund & Return Policy

At NepJewls, we take immense pride in the craftsmanship and purity of our Karigar-crafted jewelry. Because our pieces are meticulously handcrafted in Kathmandu, please review our strict return policies below.

## 1. Eligibility for Returns
We accept returns within **7 days** of delivery under the following conditions:
- The jewelry arrives damaged or defective upon delivery.
- The item received is significantly different from the product description.
- The hallmark certification is missing or incorrect.

## 2. Non-Returnable Items
Due to the bespoke nature of our jewelry, we **do not** accept returns or exchanges for:
- Custom-made or personalized pieces (e.g., custom engravings, specific sizings).
- Items showing signs of wear, alteration, or damage caused by the customer.
- "Change of mind" purchases, as precious metals are subject to daily market rate fluctuations.

## 3. Handmade Variances
Please note that because every piece is forged by hand by our local artisans, minor variations in texture, weight, or symmetry are natural and represent the authentic character of handmade jewelry. These slight deviations are not considered defects and do not qualify for a refund.

## 4. The Return Process
To initiate a return:
1. Contact our support team via WhatsApp or at support@nepjewls.com within 48 hours of receiving your package.
2. Provide your order number, clear photographs of the defect/issue, and a description of the problem.
3. If approved, we will provide instructions for securely returning the item. You are responsible for the return shipping costs unless the error was strictly on our end.

## 5. Refunds
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed back to your original payment method (eSewa, Khalti, or Fonepay) within 5-7 business days.
