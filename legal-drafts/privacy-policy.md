# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

NepJewls ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase from our store.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Personal Information:** When you make a purchase, we collect your name, billing address, shipping address, email, and phone number.
- **Payment Information:** Your payment details are processed securely through our third-party gateway partners (eSewa, Khalti, or Fonepay via PayBridge). We do not store your raw banking or wallet credentials on our servers.
- **Device Information:** We automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, and time zone, through standard cookies and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To fulfill and deliver your orders (including sharing shipping details with our local courier partners in Nepal).
- To communicate with you regarding your order status, custom inquiries, or customer service requests (including via WhatsApp).
- To screen orders for potential fraud or risk.
- To improve and optimize our website and user experience.

## 3. Sharing Your Information
We share your Personal Information with trusted third parties to help us operate our business:
- **Shopify:** Powers our online store.
- **PayBridge / Payment Providers:** Facilitates secure transactions.
- **Analytics Providers:** Google Analytics and Meta Pixel help us understand how customers use the site.

We may also share your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations in Nepal, or to respond to lawful requests for information.

## 4. Your Rights
You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us.

## 5. Contact Us
For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at privacy@nepjewls.com.
